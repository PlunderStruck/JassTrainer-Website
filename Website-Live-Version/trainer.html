Juke and Skill Shot Trainer

For the best experience, use Safari and add this app to your home screen:

Tap the Share icon and then "Add to Home Screen".

Make sure your device's auto-rotate setting is on and then rotate your device when playing the game.

How to Play ×
  • Press Q, W, or E to charge and shoot an energy sphere at the robots to score.
  • Right-clicking on the screen moves the character. Dodge the projectiles and explosions to survive.
  • Press and hold the left side of the screen to activate the move controls.
  • Press and hold the right side of the screen to activate the attack controls.
  • Holding down the attack controls will charge the energy sphere for more damage.
